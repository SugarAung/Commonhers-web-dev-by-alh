Our Story

Commenhers' pillars on a simple belief: textile waste can be given a second life while creating meaningful opportunities for the community. What started as a project has grown into a sustainability company that helps organisations transform discarded materials into practical, purposeful products for many purposes.

Through upcycling, we turn any unconventional textile materials into items such as bags, pouches, sleeves, gifts, and lifestyle products suitable for any purpose, all crafted with care by our network of local makers and beneficiaries.

Join us in making sustainability more practical, inclusive, and meaningful for everyone.

Our Commitments

Giving Waste a Second Life

At Commenhers, we transform discarded materials into practical, meaningful products. From old uniforms to tents, banners and other unconventional material, we believe that there is no end to a material's journey, but only a beginning to something new.

Supporting Organisations in their Sustainable Journey

Commenhers creates greater impact through helping businesses, schools, government agencies, retailers, and brands reduce their waste and carbon footprint through upcycling. By turning their unused materials into bags, pouches, sleeves, corporate gifts, and lifestyle products, we help organisations create visible and useful outcomes from their sustainability efforts. Be it workshops or gifting, Commenhers puts corporate goals first.

Creating Meaningful Livelihoods

We are committed to creating work opportunities for the community beneficiaries, including elderly women, single mothers or stay-at-home mothers. Through sewing and crafting work, our makers earn supplementary income while contributing to a more circular economy.
