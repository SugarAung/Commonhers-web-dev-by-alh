Our Local Beneficiaries: Uplifting Communities

At Commenhers, we believe in collaborating with the community. We work with diverse communities, ranging from elderly individuals, single mothers, marginalised women, to handmake each of our products. Our makers bring rich backgrounds and skills to their craft, creating exceptional goods that support and uplift their communities.

  • Empowering makers with upskilling opportunities
  • Helping our makers achieve financial independence through meaningful work
  • Creating a space where like-minded people come together to share their passions
  • We work with social service agencies, community centers and elderly care centres to create meaningful on-the-ground impact

Hear from our Makers!

Our Makers in the Spotlight: Science Centre Fashion Show

The DFS x Artist Fashion Show at Science Centre Singapore gave our Yuhua SewMates the chance to step out of their comfort zones and explore new possibilities. Guided by Commenhers, they turned materials like curtains and chip bags into beautiful upcycled fashion pieces. This experience helped them learn, grow, and make a meaningful impact by transforming waste into art, while building confidence and stronger connections within the community.
