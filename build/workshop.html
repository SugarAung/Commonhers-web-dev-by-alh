Workshops and Talks

Commenhers facilitates workshops designed to educate participants on fabric waste reduction and upcycling, concurrently imparting fundamental sewing techniques. These workshops are purposefully structured to raise awareness regarding the significance of sustainability, while equipping individuals with the necessary skills to actively minimize their environmental footprint.

Why Choose Us

Expert Educators in Sewing and Upcycling

With a deep portfolio, Commenhers' team of instructors are unique and experienced. They have expertise from being part of our B2B productions to teaching large workshops of a wide variety of participants to guiding other makers who want to be part of our sewing community. They are passionate educators, fully equipped to train and inspire others who wish to learn the art of sewing and upcycling.

All in One Workshop

At Commenhers, we don't just offer a variety of workshops — we're all about flexibility. Whether it's denim, PVC, or any other textile, we can design a workshop that fits perfectly with your event or activity. From hands-on education to providing all the necessary machinery, we've got you covered with an all-in-one workshop experience that's as seamless as it is fun.

Workshops for Every Audience

Commenhers workshops are designed for everyone. From empowering youth with creative design thinking to inspiring corporate teams with impactful lessons on textile waste management, we cater to diverse audiences with engaging and transformative experiences.
