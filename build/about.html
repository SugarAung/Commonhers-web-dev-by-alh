About Us

The story behind Commenhers and the people who make it happen.

Our Story

Commenhers was founded with the goal of making slow fashion more accessible, without compromising on style. All of our products are 100% ethically sourced from textile recycling companies and donation drives, and handsewn with love by local housewives and tailors in small batches.
Join our revolution to make upcycling a lifestyle for all.
