About Us

About Commenhers

Commenhers is a sustainable fashion brand which aims to make upcycled apparel & accessories as a lifestyle. Upcycling involves repurposing and remodeling apparels to create something of higher quality or value than the original.

At Commenhers, we epitomize creativity intertwined with sustainability. Our team is dedicated to crafting fun yet practical designs, setting a new standard in eco-conscious fashion.

Our Team

Yanee

CEO & Co-Founder

Yanee is currently a freshman at NUS majoring in Business Administration. With a strong family emphasis on kindness and caring for others, she founded a social enterprise in Singapore focused on upcycling fabric waste into high-quality products. Yanee's mission is clear: advocate for sustainability and inspire a positive shift towards a more eco-conscious lifestyle in Singapore.
