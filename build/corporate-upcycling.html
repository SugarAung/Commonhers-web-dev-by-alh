Corporate Upcycling Services

Transform your organization's fabric waste into value. Customized consultation and execution for businesses of all sizes.

Textile Upcycling

Commenhers offers organizations comprehensive upcycling solutions for their fabric waste, facilitating the conversion of such waste into marketable items. Our range of services encompasses the entire process, starting from consultation on digital prototyping all the way through to production.

Partnership

Our Collaboration with Decathlon

Decathlon & Commenhers

With Decathlon, we launched our upcycled bag collection! Every item in this collection is 100% made from Decathlon's non-functional inventory items (such as air mattresses, kayaks, and tents), including embellishments and zippers, and hand-sewn by local beneficiaries. As a result, this is a limited edition collection with only 100 stocks ready for sale.
