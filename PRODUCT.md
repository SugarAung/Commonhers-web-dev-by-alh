# PRODUCT.md — Commenhers

## Register
brand

## Product Purpose
Commenhers is a Singapore-based social enterprise that upcycles fabric waste into high-quality products. The website is the primary marketing and e-commerce presence — it communicates mission, showcases products, and drives corporate upcycling enquiries.

## Target Users
- Singaporean consumers and gift-givers interested in sustainable, slow-fashion products
- Corporate sustainability leads looking for upcycling services, workshops, and ESG partnerships
- Community members and volunteers aligned with social enterprise values

## Brand Personality
- Grounded and purposeful — not trendy or loud
- Warm and community-oriented — people-first, not product-first
- Confident sustainability commitment without preachy tone
- Simple, clean, honest

## Color Strategy
Committed — forest green (`#2e7d32`) carries the brand. White backgrounds. Earthy warmth, not techy cool.

## Typography
Lato 400 / 700 (Google Fonts). Clean, approachable, humanist sans-serif.

## Anti-references
- Fast fashion aesthetic (H&M, Shein) — no trend-chasing
- Corporate sustainability greenwashing — no jargon-heavy language
- Overly minimal luxury (stark white + black only) — needs warmth

## Strategic Design Principles
- Content-forward: let the mission and people speak. Don't bury with decoration.
- Accessibility matters: this audience spans age groups and tech literacy
- Trust through simplicity: no dark patterns, no misleading CTAs
- Video and photo over illustration: real people, real products
